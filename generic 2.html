Google Looker Studio

It is a free web-based tool that allows you to create and share interactive dashboards and reports from your data. It offers a wide range of connectors that make it possible to connect to virtually any kind of data.
So, I began to learn depending on myself by watching YouTube channels, and I successfully created my first dashboard on it

After I log in to my Google Looker Studio account, I click on 'Create,' and then I start choosing my data source from various options such as Google Sheets, Google Analytics, MySQL, or uploading a CSV file, among many other sources that can be linked to it for creating a report. So, I click on Google Sheets, which is my dataset, and begin my analysis

I begin to choose the right visuals to create a creative dashboard for my analysis, such as cards, textboxes, slicers, line charts, bar charts, maps, and tables. I am still enhancing my skills in this area.
