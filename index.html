My First Project
Restaurant Analysis Report

Power BI is a powerful business intelligence tool that empowers users to visualize and analyze data in a highly interactive and intuitive manner. To discover this powerful tool, I completed a course in Power BI at@Nautika.Co
also earning@ My certificate from Nautika.Co and begin worked on a simple data set for a restaurant and initiated the analysis of this data, presenting it in a report. In this project, I integrated Power BI with Power Point by designing the background in Power Point and incorporating it into Power BI to create a creative dashboard. I utilized the TOPN DAX function to obtain the top-ranked item (City Code) since my dataset lacks city names, making it impractical to use a filter. I implemented three slicers for efficient data analysis and used cards to display important information. For sales analysis, I employed a Line chart and leveraged new Power BI updates such as Subtitle to include crucial data like total sales and changes in sales numbers over the years. Additionally, I used the Format DAX function to incorporate Growth Sale information. To analyze sales data over different time frames (Year, Quarter, Month, Day), I employed Bookmarks.
